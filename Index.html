Project NP

Rational Reconstruction & $p$-adic Lifting for LWE Cryptanalysis

Abstract

Project NP introduces a deterministic framework for resolving Learning With Errors (LWE) secrets by treating noise as periodic 2-adic rational fractions. Developed by Marilyn Hsiao at NTNU, this engine utilizes a recursive parity-lifting formula to "bridge" the gap between Truth ($P$) and Falsehood ($N$).

The Mathematical Core

$$NP_{s+1} = \frac{NP_s + P_s + N_s}{2^{\text{v}_2(P_s \oplus N_s)}}$$

In partnership with the Open Experiments Community.
